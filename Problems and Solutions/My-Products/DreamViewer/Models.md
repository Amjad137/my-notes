## 🎥 AI Video Generation (Free or Freemium)

### 🟡 **Runway ML**

* 📌 Free tier with monthly credits (e.g., ~125 credits) for **text-to-video + image-to-video**.
* Works well for short clips with motion and effects. ([Gadgets 360][1])

### 🟡 **Pika Labs**

* Free monthly credits for **AI video creation** with stylized visuals and effects.
* Good for creative short clips. ([DevStackTips][2])

### 🟡 **Luma Dream Machine**

* Offers free video generations (limited number per month).
* Creates short videos with motion and animation. ([SIMA Labs][3])

### 🟡 **Google AI Studio (Gemini / Veo)**

* Free access to Google’s AI tools including basic video generation via the **AI Studio** web IDE.
* Video features may be limited or promotional but *do exist in the free tier*. ([Wikipedia][4])

📌 **Note:** Truly free unlimited video generation is rare — most platforms give only credits or limited outputs per period. ([Reddit][5])

## 🖼 AI Image Generation (Free / Credits)

### 🟢 **Canva AI (Magic Studio)**

* Free Canva accounts include AI-generated images and basic video features.
* Great for social media graphics & templates. ([Onassis Krown][6])

### 🟢 **Freepik AI Video Generator**

### 🟢 **Pixwit AI**

## 🎵 AI Audio Generation (Free / Limited)

### 🟡 **Suno AI**

* Text-to-music generation with free daily quotas (e.g., ~10 songs per day).
* Great for generating music with vocals and beats. ([Onassis Krown][6])

### 🟢 **Canva AI**

* Includes basic audio tools like text-to-speech and voiceovers in its free tier. ([Onassis Krown][6])

## 📱 Free Built-In Options (via search/mobile)

### 🟡 **Bing Video Creator**

* Uses OpenAI’s **Sora** model to generate **very short videos (e.g., 5-second vertical clips)** for free via the Bing app.
* Limited number of free uses, but accessible without a paid plan. ([The Verge][9])
