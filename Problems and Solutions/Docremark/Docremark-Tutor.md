## **[SECTION 3: TEAM MANAGEMENT & ROLES - 3:30-5:15]**

**[Navigate to Members/Team section]**

"One of Docremark's strongest features is flexible team management. As an Organization Owner or Admin, you can invite team members and assign them specific roles based on their responsibilities.

**[Show Members List view]**

We support three organization roles:

- **Owner**: Full administrative access—manage the entire organization, billing, team, and all patient records
- **Doctor**: Medical professional access—create, view, and manage patient records
- **Staff**: Administrative support—assist with scheduling, patient data entry, and organization management

**[Show the Pending Invitations interface]**

When you invite team members, they receive an invitation that expires after a set period. New members can accept or decline invitations from their dashboard. This ensures smooth onboarding without cluttering the system with unused accounts."

- Medical ID numbers

Within each patient profile, authorized users can view complete medical history, manage appointments, and track patient progress. The system ensures that only authorized team members can access sensitive patient data based on their role.

**[Show create patient flow]**

Creating a new patient is straightforward. Enter their demographic information, and the system automatically validates and stores it securely. Unique constraints prevent duplicate patient records, maintaining data quality."

## **Tips for Recording:**
- **Cursor speed**: Slow down your mouse movements for clarity
- **Zoom in**: Make sure text is readable (150-200% zoom if needed)
- **Natural pace**: Pause briefly at each section so viewers can absorb information
- **Use annotations**: Add text overlays for key features or statistics
- **Highlight interactions**: Click slowly through forms and workflows to show the user experience
- **Background music**: Add subtle, professional background music to keep energy up
