
# 🧠 Big Picture: What exists in Software Design?

Think of it like this:

## 🏗️ 3 Layers of Knowledge

---

## 🟣 1. Principles (HOW to think)

These are **rules/guidelines**

### 🔥 Core Principles

* **SOLID**

  * Single Responsibility
  * Open/Closed
  * Liskov Substitution
  * Interface Segregation
  * Dependency Inversion

* **DRY** → Don’t Repeat Yourself

* **KISS** → Keep It Simple

* **YAGNI** → You Aren’t Gonna Need It

👉 These guide your decisions

---

## 🟢 2. Design Patterns (HOW to structure code)

Reusable solutions to common problems

---

### 🔥 Creational (object creation)

* Singleton
* Factory
* Builder

---

### 🔥 Structural (how things are connected)

* Adapter
* Decorator
* Facade

---

### 🔥 Behavioral (how things interact)

* Strategy ✅ (VERY important for you)
* Observer
* Command

---

---

## 🟡 3. Architecture Patterns (SYSTEM level)

* MVC
* Layered Architecture
* Microservices
* Clean Architecture

👉 This is **big-picture design**
